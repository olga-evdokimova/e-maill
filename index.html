DOWNLOAD THE APP TO RECEIVE YOUR TICKETS:

Download the app by searching “Emirates Dubai 7s” in the Apple App Store or Google Play.

If you have the app from last year, please update it.

Create an account upon opening the app. Please note that you must use the same email address that this invite has been sent to.

If you have an account from last year, you can re-use it, if your email has not changed. If it has, you must create a new account.

In case you forgot your password, you can recover it by clicking “forgot password” at the login page within the app.

ACСEPT TICKETS IN THE APP

Upon successful login, you will receive a prompt on screen advising that a ticket or several tickets have been assigned to you..

Please click “accept”.

Please note that tickets are non-transferable.

ON THE DAY:

If you follow the steps above, your ticket will be available in the application.

Upon arrival at the venue, please enter via the VIP entrance and proceed to the Heineken Silver Welcome Booth.

Present your ticket in the app ‘wallet’ and have the QR code scanned to gain entry.

Upon registration, you will receive a Heineken Silver VIP rugby shirt, which must be worn at all times with in the Heineken Silver VIP Chalet & Grandstand.

Kindly note that the Heineken Silver Welcome Booth will only be open from 10 a.m. to 5 p.m. We are unable to grant access outside of these hours.

If you leave items at the Heineken Silver Welcome Booth, please retain your cloakroom ticket to reclaim your items from our office after the event (if you cannot collect them before closing).

The Grandstand has limited seating. Therefore, we encourage you to secure your seat prior to the games to avoid disappointment.

Please note that we are unable to host any persons under 21 years of age in the Heineken Silver VIP Chalet or Grandstand.

Please enjoy responsibly and do not drink and drive. Public transport will be available at the event.
